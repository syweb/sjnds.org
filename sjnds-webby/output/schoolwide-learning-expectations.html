SJNDS students will be people of:

Faith

Faith who participate in the worship of the faith community, understand and apply Church teachings, live the Gospel by serving others, and joyfully respect Godâ€™s gifts of life and creation.

Action

Action who confidently set their goals and work as a team. Our students will apply their knowledge to produce quality solutions and products and will use technology appropriately. SJNDS students will learn to adapt to change.

Integrity

Integrity who communicate compassionately, honestly, and effectively and who evaluate their choices in light of faith.

Thought

Thought who gather information effectively, think critically, generate solutions creatively, and evaluate morally.

Healthy Lifestyles

Healthy Lifestyles who make choices reflecting knowledge of health and fitness and who participate in social and recreational activities. Our students will be aware of the environment and global issues that affect us. Students will appreciate, learn, and participate in the visual and performing arts.
