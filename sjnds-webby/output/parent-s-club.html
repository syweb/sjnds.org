Parents’ Clubs serve an essential role in Catholic Schools. The Catholic School Department of the Diocese of Sacramento directs Parents’ Clubs to fulfill the following tasks in this order of importance:

  • To promote open communication among the parents, teachers, and administration to aid in all aspects of education, growth, and development
  • To build community and cooperation among the parents, faculty, administration, Advisory Commission, and the parish of SJNDS
  • To direct and coordinate parental support to SJNDS through assistance activities, social functions, and fund-raisers.

The SJNDS Parents’ Club continues to be one of the most successful Parents’ Clubs in the Sacramento Diocese in their fund-raising efforts.
