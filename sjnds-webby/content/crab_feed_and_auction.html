Crab Feed and Auction

It is here, THE event of the year! The Crab Feed and Auction are the biggest fund raisers at SJNDS and a not to be missed event. The food is wonderful, especially the homemade minestrone soup and the endless crab. After food comes fun, with the live auction. Where else can you win a shopping trip with Mrs. Curtis, a Giants game with Mrs. Hurtado and Mrs. Schneider or a Mexican Fiesta for thirty friends?!
