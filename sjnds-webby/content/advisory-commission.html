Sharing responsibility with the Bishop, the pastor of St. John the Baptist Parish establishes the Commission as the policy making body in matters of Catholic school education. The Commission shall be responsible to the pastor for achieving diocesan and parish goals for Catholic education, for reviewing and approving the budget, for determining sources for funding, and for reporting on the status of Catholic school education in the parish.
