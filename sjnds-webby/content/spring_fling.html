Spring Fling

"Let’s dance! Put on your red shoes and dance!" Come see each class tap their toes and swing their hips as they dance to one of the latest pop hits. You’ll cheer for the cute kindergarteners hip hopping to the beat and get teary eyed over the eighth graders waltzing with their parent. And the rest of the classes can really "shake their groove thing."
