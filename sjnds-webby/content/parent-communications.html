Parent communication is important at SJNDS. The faculty strives to keep families informed of current events and activities in the classrooms.

SJNDS utilizes a weekly Family Envelope system for communication between school and parents/guardians and a weekly e-mailed newsletter. Each Thursday the Family Envelope is sent home with the youngest or only student in each family and should be returned to school by the following day.
