In order for the Parents’ Club to meet its financial obligation to the school, each family must earn 40 points (minimum 10 points in fund-raising) in the Parent Involvement Program (PIP) for the period May 1st through April 30. Only Parents’ Club sanctioned events/functions will earn credit toward the PIP Point System. PIP Points can be earned by purchasing Scrip according to the schedule published prior to registration. In order for the Parents’ Club to meet its financial obligation to the school, children will not be registered for the following year if the commitments agreed to in the Tuition/Parent Involvement Program Contract for the current year are not fulfilled.
