SJNDS provides frequent opportunities for the reception of the sacraments. During the second grade, children prepare for and receive both the Sacraments of Reconciliation and Eucharist. During the eighth grade, students prepare for and receive the Sacrament of Confirmation. During Advent and Lent students in grades 3-8 have the opportunity to receive the Sacrament of Reconciliation.
