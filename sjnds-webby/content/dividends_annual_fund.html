Annual Fund

The focus for this year's "Dividends for Life" annual fund will be Athletics. It is our goal to replace all four basketball goal areas on the playground and to purchase a new scoreboard for the Parish Hall. Additional funds will be used to support other programs where the need is greatest.

Pledge cards will be sent home through the Family Envelope on November 10. Turn in pledge cards at our "interesting" carline on Friday, November 19. Contact the school office for more information.
