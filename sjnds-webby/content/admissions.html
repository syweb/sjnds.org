SJNDS in the Diocese of Sacramento, mindful of its primary mission to be witnesses to the love of Christ for all, admits students of any race, color, and national and/or ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at all schools. SJNDS does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and/or ethnic origin, age, sex, or disability in the administration of educational policies, scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other school- administered programs.

Likewise, SJNDS does not discriminate against any application for employment on the basis of sex, age, disability, race, color, and national and/or ethnic origin. Please click on Admission Guidelines for more detailed information.
